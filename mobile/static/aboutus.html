cover sun company

Cover Sun is a specialized manufacturer of all kinds of modern curtains and electric ceilings, which started its activity in 2012, and after two decades of efforts and focusing on innovation in this field, this company has been able to improve and supply the quality level of this branch of the industry. A comprehensive package of different products that meets all the needs of modern projects in this field. It is worth mentioning that due to its project-oriented approach, Cover Sun's warehouse always has a significant inventory of various fabric accessories and required engines, which has made it very competitive in terms of project implementation speed. With an experienced team, Cover Sun has satisfied hundreds of customers by successfully completing numerous large projects, all under the company's warranty and continuous service. Undoubtedly, it is one of the leading and reliable companies in this field
